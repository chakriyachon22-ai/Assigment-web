About Us

Welcome to our Battambang Travel Guide! We are passionate about showcasing the beauty, culture, and history of Battambang Province - one of Cambodia's most charming and authentic destinations.

Our Mission

To help travelers discover the hidden gems of Battambang and experience the authentic Cambodian culture, while supporting local communities and sustainable tourism.

Why Battambang?

Battambang is Cambodia's second-largest city, yet it retains a peaceful, small-town atmosphere that makes it the perfect escape from the busier tourist destinations. With its well-preserved French colonial architecture, thriving arts scene, and stunning countryside, Battambang offers visitors an authentic glimpse into Cambodian life.

What We Offer

Our travel guide provides comprehensive information about Battambang's top attractions, including ancient temples, natural wonders, cultural experiences, and local cuisine. Whether you're interested in history, nature, adventure, or simply soaking up the laid-back atmosphere, we've got you covered.

Featured Destinations

Sustainable Tourism

We believe in responsible travel that benefits local communities. When you visit Battambang, you're not just seeing beautiful sights - you're supporting local artisans, farmers, and small business owners who keep Cambodia's cultural heritage alive.

Our Team

Our team consists of local guides and travel enthusiasts who have deep roots in Battambang Province. We know the best spots, the hidden gems, and the stories behind every location. Our passion is sharing this beautiful region with the world while preserving its authenticity and charm.

Did You Know?

Battambang means "Loss of Stick" in Khmer, referring to a legend about a king who lost his magical stick in the area. Today, the city's emblem features this legendary stick!
